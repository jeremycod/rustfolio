# Rustfolio Tutorial – Market Regime

> Audience: Beginners who want to understand market conditions  
> Goal: Understand current macro market environment.

---

# 📈 What Is Market Regime?

Market regime identifies the current market environment:

- Bull Market (rising, low volatility)
- Normal Market (stable)
- Bear Market (falling, high volatility)

It answers:

> "What kind of market are we in right now?"

# 📊 Regime Forecast

Shows probability of:

- Bull Market
- Normal Market
- Bear Market

Over next 10–30 days.

Example:

```
Bull: 25%
Normal: 60%
Bear: 15%
```

---

# 🧠 Why Regime Matters

High volatility regime:
Reduce risk exposure.

Normal regime:
Maintain standard allocation.

Bull regime:
Growth assets may outperform.

---

# 📉 Regime-Based Strategy

### Bull Market
- Increase growth stocks
- Higher beta positions
- Momentum strategies

### Normal Market
- Balanced allocation
- Standard diversification
- Moderate risk

### Bear Market
- Defensive positions
- Lower beta stocks
- Capital preservation

# ⚠️ Important Reminder

Regime detection is probabilistic, not certain.

Use it as one input among many.

Don't make drastic changes based solely on regime.

# 🔄 When To Review Market Regime

- Before increasing position sizes
