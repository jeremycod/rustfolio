# Rustfolio Tutorial – Trading Signals

> Audience: Beginners who want technical indicators  
> Goal: Generate technical-based buy/sell indicators.

---

# 📊 What Are Trading Signals?

Trading signals are technical indicators suggesting:

- Buy opportunities
- Sell opportunities
- Hold recommendations

Based on:
- Price patterns
- Momentum
- Mean reversion
- Trend analysis

---

# 🔍 Signal Types

## Momentum Signal

Identifies when price momentum is strong.

Example:

```
BUY - Probability: 72%
```

Momentum signals work best in trending markets.

---

## Mean Reversion Signal

Identifies when price has moved too far from average.

Example:

```
SELL - Probability: 65%
```

Mean reversion works best in range-bound markets.

---

## Trend Signal

Identifies overall trend direction.

Example:

```
BUY - Probability: 68%
```

Trend signals work best in sustained moves.

---

## Combined Signal

Aggregates all signals into one recommendation.

Example:

```
BUY - Probability: 70%
```

Most reliable when multiple signals agree.

---

# 📈 Signal Strength

Each signal includes probability estimate:

- 50-60%: Weak signal
- 60-70%: Moderate signal
- 70-80%: Strong signal
- 80%+: Very strong signal

Higher probability = more confidence.

---

# ⚠️ Important Warning

Signals are informational only.

They are NOT:
- Financial advice
- Guaranteed predictions
- Standalone decision tools

Use with:
- Risk analysis
- Market context
- Position sizing discipline
- Your investment strategy

---

# 🧠 How Beginners Should Use Signals

Step 1:
Check combined signal first

Step 2:
Review individual signal types

Step 3:
Look at probability

Step 4:
Combine with risk analysis

Step 5:
Never act on signals alone

---

# 📊 Signal Conflicts

Sometimes signals disagree:

- Momentum: BUY
- Mean Reversion: SELL
- Trend: HOLD

This indicates:
Market uncertainty or transition phase.

When signals conflict:
Exercise extra caution.

---

# 🔄 When To Review Trading Signals

- Before entering new positions
- When considering exits
- During earnings season
- After major news events
- Weekly portfolio review

---

# ⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes

❌ Following signals blindly  
❌ Ignoring risk metrics  
❌ Over-trading based on signals  
❌ Not considering market context  
❌ Using signals without stop-losses

- Should I exit this position?

---

# 📌 Signal + Risk = Better Decisions

Good signal + High risk = Proceed with caution

Good signal + Low risk = More favorable setup

Bad signal + High risk = Avoid

Bad signal + Low risk = May still be acceptable

---

# 📌 Final Takeaway

Trading Signals tell you:
> "What technical patterns suggest"

Risk Analysis tells you:
> "How much you could lose"

Combine both for informed decisions.
